Listen Up!

Share SoundCloud playlists with your friends or play and edit them with a group of people.

Create a playlist »

Create

Start a playlist by adding tracks that you already know, by searching SoundCloud or by choosing from a list of trending tracks (courtesy of TrendSpottr).

Share

Send your playlist to your friends, co-workers so that they can add tracks of their own.

Listen

A shared playlist can be played in realtime on multiple devices!
